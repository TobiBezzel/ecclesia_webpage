About the App

Ecclesia Companion is a purpose-built mobile app for people who want to engage deeply with Sunday services, sermons, and their personal faith journey. Whether you're a seasoned churchgoer or just beginning to explore, Ecclesia helps you capture thoughts during the message, pray consistently throughout the week, take on personal challenges, and carry every Sunday's insight into your daily life.

Smart Note Editor

Free-form or guided capture with structured anchors.

Prayer Journal

Track requests, set reminders, enter Pray Mode.

Weekly Challenges

One concrete commitment per week, tracked daily.

Note Library

Search, filter by tag, folder, or date.

Cloud Sync

Access your data from any device, securely.

Privacy First

Your data stays yours. No ads, no selling data.
