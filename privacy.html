General Information on Data Processing

Protecting your personal data is important to us. We process your data exclusively based on the legal provisions of the GDPR and the German Telemedia Act (TMG). This privacy policy informs you about the most important aspects of data processing within our app and on this website.

3
Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal bases:

  • Art. 6 (1) b GDPR – Contract performance: Providing and using app functions
  • Art. 6 (1) a GDPR – Consent: Where you have explicitly given consent
  • Art. 6 (1) f GDPR – Legitimate interests: App improvement, troubleshooting, and security
4
Data We Collect
4.1 Account & Profile Data
  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Optional profile information: display name, bio, city, church, phone number
  • Profile picture (if uploaded by you)
4.2 App Content Data
  • Sermon notes and documents you create
  • Prayer requests and their status
  • Weekly challenges and progress
  • One-step commitments
  • App settings and preferences (e.g. notification times, theme)
4.3 Technical & Usage Data
  • App usage patterns and features used (anonymized analytics)
  • Performance and crash reports
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
5
Purpose of Data Processing
  • Providing and maintaining Ecclesia Companion services
  • User authentication and account management
  • Syncing your data across devices (via Firebase)
  • Sending notification reminders you have scheduled
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Ensuring security and preventing misuse
6
Data Storage & Security
6.1 Cloud Storage (Firebase)

Your data — including notes, prayers, and challenges — is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), which is operated by Google LLC. Firebase complies with GDPR and uses EU Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable. Your authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication.

6.2 Local Storage

In addition to cloud storage, certain data is cached locally on your device for offline access and performance. This includes your notes, settings, and preferences.

6.3 Storage Duration
  • User account data: Until your account is deleted
  • Technical logs: Up to 90 days
  • Support requests: 3 years after completion
6.4 Security Measures

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from loss, unauthorized access, and misuse. Data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

7
Disclosure of Data to Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Data may be shared only in the following limited cases:

  • With your explicit prior consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • To Google Firebase (as our cloud infrastructure provider), under a data processing agreement
  • To protect the rights and security of the developer and users
  • In the event of a business transfer (e.g. merger), you will be notified in advance
8
Third-Party Services

Ecclesia Companion uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) – Secure login via email/password or Google Sign-In
  • Cloud Firestore (Google LLC) – Secure cloud storage for your app data
  • Expo Notifications – Local push notification scheduling (no data sent to third parties)

Google's privacy policy applies to Firebase services: policies.google.com/privacy

9
Your Rights (Art. 15–22 GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15): Request information about your stored data
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16): Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your data ("Right to be forgotten"). You can delete your account directly in the app under Settings → Delete Account.
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18): Restrict processing of your data
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object (Art. 21): Object to data processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7 (3)): Withdraw any consent given at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact: ecclesia.companion@proton.me

ecclesia.companion@proton.me
