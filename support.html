Frequently Asked Questions
How do I create a new sermon note?

Tap the "New Note" button on the Home screen. You can choose between Free Notes (open canvas) or Guided Capture (structured anchors for aha moments, prayers, questions, and action steps). Give your document a title and start writing.

Can I access my notes offline?

Yes! Ecclesia Companion caches your notes locally on your device. You can read and work with previously loaded content offline. New content you create while offline will sync the next time you have an internet connection.

How do I add a prayer request?

Navigate to the Prayer tab. Type your request in the input field at the top and tap the + button. You can also add a description, tags, and set a reminder notification. Tap the bell icon on any prayer to schedule it.

What is "Pray Mode"?

Pray Mode is a focused, distraction-free prayer session. Tap the "Start Pray Mode" button in the Prayer tab. You'll be guided through each of your active prayer requests one by one — you can mark them as prayed, skip, or discard them.

How do weekly challenges work?

In the Week tab, tap "New Challenge" and write one concrete commitment for the week. Each day you complete it, tap that day's circle to check it off. After the week, archive the challenge to see your history.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to your Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data from our servers. This action cannot be undone. Alternatively, contact us at ecclesia@proton.me to request manual deletion.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes. Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell or share your personal data. There are no ads in the app. You can read our full Privacy Policy for all details.

How do I search my notes in the Library?

From the Home screen, tap "Library". Use the search bar to search by title. Use the filter panel to narrow by tag, folder, or date range. Results update instantly as you type.

Tips & Tricks

Use Guided Capture anchors

In Guided Mode, use the colored buttons (Aha, Prayer, Question, Application) to route each thought. This makes your summary screen much more useful after the service.

Tag your documents

Use tags like "Sermon", "Youth", "Small Group" when creating notes. They make filtering in the Library much faster.

Set individual prayer reminders

Go to Settings → Prayer Reminders and switch to "Per Prayer" mode. Then set custom times for each prayer request individually.

Save a One-Step commitment

On the Summary screen after a session, save a "One Step" — one concrete action for the week. It appears on your home screen as a visual reminder throughout the week.
