Features

Everything you need to grow from Sunday

Purpose-built tools that help you reflect, remember, and respond — every day of the week.

Smart Note Editor
Capture sermons in Free Mode (open canvas) or Guided Mode with structured anchors for aha moments, prayers, questions, and action steps.
Prayer Journal
Add prayer requests, mark them answered, set flexible reminders, and enter focused Pray Mode for an undistracted prayer session.
Weekly Challenges
Set one concrete challenge per week. Check off days, track progress, and build lasting habits one Sunday at a time.
Library & Search
All your past notes organized by folder, tag, or date. Powerful filtering so you can always find what you captured weeks ago.
Cloud Sync
Everything syncs securely across devices so your notes and prayers are always with you, whether on your phone or tablet.
Dark & Light Mode
A refined interface that adapts to your preference, crafted for late-night reflections and bright Sunday mornings alike.
